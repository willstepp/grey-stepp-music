A Brief History
 
I grew up in north Georgia and started playing music in high school.
 
I moved to Nashville while playing with the band Waiting For December back in 2002.  After the band parted ways in 2003, I interned with Donnie Boutwell at The Park Studio and started learning engineering and producing.
 
In 2006 I decided to go to school at SAE Institute in Nashville to further my knowledge of engineering and graduated in 2007, 2nd in my class.
 
I spent the rest of that year touring with Eleisha Eagle after engineering on her record (produced by Donnie Boutwell), then came home and got a job. I’ve been doing random studio work and playing live with various bands, mostly Patrick Collum & The Law, since then.
 
I got married in the Fall of 2008, and I live in West Nashville with my wife Shannon, our cat, Joey, and our 2 dogs, Hank and Lila.
